Dashboard

Home base

Your personalized overview showing total tokens, vehicles owned, quick-access stats, and recent activity — everything at a glance.

Cars

Full catalog

Browse every car in the database with powerful search and category filters. Click any card to open a detailed spec modal with horsepower, torque, top speed, and more.

Aircraft

Aviation section

Dedicated aircraft catalog with the same rich detail — range, ceiling, engine type, and capacity. Filter by category and search by name or model.

Spend your earned tokens to purchase vehicles for your personal garage. Each item shows its token cost, specs preview, and ownership status.

Garage

View every vehicle you've purchased. Your personal fleet displayed in a clean grid with full specs and an "Owned" badge on each card.
